Who is B33BOP?

B33Bop ([Βí·Bǫp]) is a friendly short alien who arrived on Earth on April 9th, early in the morning. He comes from a planet beyond the Horsehead Nebula, which is also known as B33. B33Bop left his home planet because there were no games or social media there. He's here to have fun and make a lot of friends. B33Bop loves playing games and wants to share the joy of gaming with everyone he meets! He may not be the brightest intergalactic traveler on Earth, but he is the cutest.
